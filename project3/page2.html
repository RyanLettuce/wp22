Mushroom Cards

A series of 21 cards translating the Wikipedia article "Mushroom" into a new media and/or means of consumption. Using the classic look of collectible sports cards, this deck aims to educate, with stylized but accurate illustration and a scientific naming scheme.

In the first phase, three posters were extrapolated from the original to create a cohesive triplet. In the second and third phases, additional material was created to suppliment this new identity, including a 10 second animation and pins (see below).

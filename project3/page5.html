CA Infographic

An 18"x24" Infographic poster displaying data and information regarding water scarcity in southern California. Using a series of maps, figures, and graphs, it attempts to clearly describe a growing crisis in the American southwest.

Using a simple but playful color palette, this graphic displays a series of sub-graphics which derive their facts and figures from sites like the Metropolitan Water District of Southern California (see below).

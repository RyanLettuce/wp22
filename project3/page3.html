Library of Congress

A re-designed identity for the Library of Congress, the oldest cultural institution in the United States, and the largest library in the world. Building off previous identities, this re-design focuses in on a rebranding through the web, UI/UX, and wayfinding. See the style guide here.

In the first phase, three posters were extrapolated from the original to create a cohesive triplet. In the second and third phases, additional material was created to suppliment this new identity, including a 10 second animation and pins (see below).

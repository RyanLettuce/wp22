Information

I am an interdisciplinary designer currently receiving my BFA in graphic design from the Rhode Island School of Design.

I'm interested in playful, interactive design, with a range of curiousities, working within a variety of digital and analogue programs and media to acheive any task given or taken on.

Legos, Minecraft, and Star Wars are some core pillars of my childhood (and my current inspiration). I love movies, anything made out of blocks, and building and making new things.

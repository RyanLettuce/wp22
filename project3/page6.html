No Signal

A series of 3 posters containing connecting imagery extrapolated into a larger project creating other media inside of an identity. These include the 3 original posters, font specimen, a GIF, and button pins. My interpretation of the project revolved around the phrase No Signal, a glitching effect, as well as images taken of various textures.

In the first phase, three posters were extrapolated from the original to create a cohesive triplet. In the second and third phases, additional material was created to suppliment this new identity, including a 10 second animation and pins (see below).
